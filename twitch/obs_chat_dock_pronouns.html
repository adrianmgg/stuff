Pronouns Extension in OBS Chat Dock

by amgg

  1. open obs’s settings (File→Settings)
  2. go to the Stream category and select FrankerFaceZ from the Twitch Chat Add-Ons dropdown, then click Ok to save the settings
  3. enable the chat dock (View→Docks→Chat)
  4. open the settings page in the chat dock (the gear icon), switch to non-mod settings, and select FrankerFaceZ Control Center
  5. in the newly opened ffz control center window, go to the Add-Ons section, find the “Pronouns” extension, and enable it.
